For freshers in India, here are some simple investment options:
- Systematic Investment Plan (SIP): You invest a fixed amount of money in mutual funds every month. It's a great way to start disciplined investing with a small amount.
- Public Provident Fund (PPF): A long-term, government-backed savings scheme with tax benefits. The lock-in period is 15 years.
- Tax-Saver Mutual Funds (ELSS): These are mutual funds that come with a tax benefit under section 80C of the Income Tax Act. They have a lock-in period of 3 years.
